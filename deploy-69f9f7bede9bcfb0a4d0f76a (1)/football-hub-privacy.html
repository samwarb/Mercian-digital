Football Hub Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 May 2026

← Back to Home

Football Hub is a football information app that provides fixtures, standings, scores, squads, player information, statistics, news, and TV listing information.

We respect your privacy. Football Hub does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information to use the app.

Information We Collect

Football Hub does not collect personal information directly from users.

The app does not use advertising tracking, analytics tracking, location tracking, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or other device permissions.

Technical Information

Football Hub connects to our backend service to load football data, news, scores, fixtures, standings, and related information.

Like most internet services, our hosting provider may process basic technical information when requests are made, such as IP address, request time, browser or device information, and server logs. This information is used for service reliability, security, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.

We do not use this information to identify individual users, create user profiles, or sell personal data.

Third-Party Data Sources

Football Hub displays information from third-party football, news, and sports data sources. These may include football data providers, news publishers, broadcasters, and publicly available football feeds.

When you open a news article or external source, that website may collect information according to its own privacy policy. Football Hub is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Email Support

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. We use this only to respond to your support request.

Our support email is:

footballhub@merciandigital.co.uk

Data Sharing

Football Hub does not sell personal information.

We do not share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Retention

Football Hub does not create user accounts or store personal profiles.

Support emails may be retained for customer service and record-keeping purposes.

Children’s Privacy

Football Hub is a general sports information app. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

footballhub@merciandigital.co.uk
