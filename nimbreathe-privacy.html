Nimbreathe Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

← Back to Home

Nimbreathe is a breathing and mindfulness app that provides guided breathing sessions, reminders, optional Apple Health integration, and optional in-app support purchases.

We respect your privacy. Nimbreathe does not require an account or login to use the app.

Information We Collect

Nimbreathe does not collect personal information directly into a user account and does not require you to register.

The app may ask for your first name during onboarding. This is used to personalise the app experience, such as greetings and reminder wording.

Nimbreathe does not use advertising tracking, third-party analytics tracking, location tracking, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera permissions.

Information Stored On Your Device

Nimbreathe may store app preferences locally on your device, including your first name, onboarding status, selected breathing preferences, reminder settings, and app settings.

This information is used to make the app work properly and provide a more personal experience.

Notifications

If you choose to enable reminders, Nimbreathe will ask for notification permission. Reminder settings are used to schedule local notifications on your device.

You can change notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Apple Health

If you grant permission, Nimbreathe may write completed breathing sessions to Apple Health as mindful minutes.

Apple Health access is optional. You can manage or revoke Health permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or iOS Settings.

Nimbreathe only uses Apple Health permissions for the wellbeing features described in the app.

In-App Purchases

Nimbreathe may offer optional support purchases, such as “coffee” tips, using Apple In-App Purchase.

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Email Support

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. We use this only to respond to your support request.

Our support email is:

nimbreathe@merciandigital.co.uk

Data Sharing

Nimbreathe does not sell personal information.

We do not share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Retention

Nimbreathe does not create user accounts or store personal profiles on our servers.

Local app settings remain on your device unless you delete or reset the app.

Support emails may be retained for customer service and record-keeping purposes.

Children’s Privacy

Nimbreathe is a general wellbeing app. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

nimbreathe@merciandigital.co.uk
