FAQ

What is Football Hub?

Football Hub is a football companion app that brings together fixtures, league tables, live scores, squads, player information, stats, news, and TV listing information in one place.

Is Football Hub an official football app?

No. Football Hub is an independent app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Premier League, EFL, clubs, players, broadcasters, or news providers.

Do I need an account?

No. Football Hub does not require an account or login.

Does Football Hub track me?

No. Football Hub does not use advertising tracking, analytics tracking, or third-party tracking SDKs.

How do I report a problem?

Email footballhub@merciandigital.co.uk with a short description of the issue. If possible, include the team, match, screen, or news article where you noticed the problem.

Why is a score, fixture, or table sometimes delayed?

Football Hub relies on external football data sources. We aim to show useful and timely information, but some updates may be delayed, incomplete, or temporarily unavailable.

Why is TV information sometimes missing?

TV listings are provided on a best-effort basis and may change close to kickoff. Always check your broadcaster before making viewing plans.

Why is some squad or player information incomplete?

Squad and player data depends on the availability and quality of third-party sources. Some competitions and teams may have more complete data than others.

Why does a news article open from another publisher?

Football Hub shows football headlines and summaries from third-party publishers. Full articles remain with the original publisher and may be subject to that publisher’s own terms and privacy policy.

Known Limitations

  • Live scores may be delayed depending on the availability and refresh rate of external data sources.
  • Fixtures, standings, squads, player stats, and TV listings may occasionally be incomplete or incorrect.
  • TV channel information is best-effort and should be checked with the broadcaster before relying on it.
  • News filtering may occasionally include articles that are only loosely related to a team, player, or competition.
  • Player photos, squad lists, and transfer changes may not update immediately.
  • Championship data may be less complete than Premier League data depending on source availability.
  • Football Hub is not a betting, gambling, ticketing, or official match information service.
  • For urgent or official information, check the relevant club, league, broadcaster, or venue directly.
